High Density Apple Plantation – Complete Guide

High density apple planting is the future of modern apple cultivation. This system improves early bearing, better light interception, uniform fruit quality and higher productivity per unit area.

Key Components:

  • Selection of dwarf rootstocks (M9, M26 etc.)
  • Proper spacing (3x1 m or as recommended)
  • Training system – Tall spindle / Slender spindle
  • Drip irrigation & fertigation management
  • Regular canopy management

Advantages:

  • Early fruiting (2–3 years)
  • Higher yield per hectare
  • Better fruit colour & size
  • Easy pest & disease management

High density orcharding requires technical precision but provides long-term economic sustainability.
