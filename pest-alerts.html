Identification:

  • Sticky honeydew secretion

White Peach Scale Alert

Scientific Name: Pseudaulacaspis pentagona

Identification:

  • White circular scale insects on bark
  • Yellowing and weakening of branches
  • Heavy infestation may cause dieback
  • Scales form dense colonies on trunk

Risk Period:

Late winter to early spring.

Immediate Action:

  • Scrape heavily infested bark during dormancy
  • Apply recommended dormant oil spray
  • Maintain orchard sanitation
