Winter Orchard Management Advisory

1️⃣ Structural Pruning

  • Remove dead, diseased and crossing branches
  • Maintain central leader structure
  • Ensure proper light penetration

2️⃣ Dormant Spray

  • Apply Bordeaux mixture or recommended fungicide
  • Use horticultural oil to control scale & overwintering pests

3️⃣ Sanitation Practices

  • Remove fallen leaves & mummified fruits
  • Destroy infected plant material

4️⃣ Soil & Nutrition

  • Apply well-decomposed FYM
  • Conduct soil testing if not done in last 2 years

5️⃣ Frost Protection

  • Whitewash trunk to prevent sunscald
  • Maintain proper irrigation before frost

Goal: Enter spring season with healthy and well-prepared orchard.
