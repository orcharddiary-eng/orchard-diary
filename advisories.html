❄ Winter Orchard Management Advisory

EN: Winter is the most important season to prepare your orchard for the next crop. Proper management improves yield and reduces pests.
HI: सर्दी का मौसम अगले सीजन की तैयारी के लिए सबसे महत्वपूर्ण होता है।

Pruning / छंटाई

Remove dead, diseased, crossing branches to improve sunlight.

Dormant Spray / डॉर्मेंट स्प्रे

Apply fungicide + horticultural oil to control overwintering pests.

Soil & Nutrition / पोषण

Apply FYM and conduct soil testing.

Sanitation / स्वच्छता

Remove fallen leaves and infected fruits.

Frost Protection / पाला सुरक्षा

Whitewash trunk, irrigate properly before frost.
